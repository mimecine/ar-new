This exhibition featured works by the internationally celebrated American artist, Bruce Nauman. Highlights include two unique neon pieces: the early work, La Brea/Art Tips/Rat Spit/Tar Pits 1972 and Trust Me Only Big Studio1984; two sculptures Untitled, Hand Circle 1996 and Partial Truth 1997; the important unique two-monitor video work, Raw Materials Washing Hands 1996; and two further videos. The exhibition took place in Tramway 5.
