Six ARTIST ROOMS including Robert Therrien’s gigantic sculpture No Title (Table and Four Chairs) 2003 filled the central gallery of a display exploring scale. Other highlights included major paintings by Ed Ruscha and Andy Warhol in the States of Flux galleries on Level 5. Works by Jannis Kounellis and Anselm Kiefer were shown in a suite of galleries dedicated to the arte povera movement.
